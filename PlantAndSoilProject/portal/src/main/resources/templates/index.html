Understanding Soil Types

Soil is a vital component for plant growth. Different plants thrive in different soil types. Here's an overview:

  • Sandy Soil: Drains quickly but doesn't hold nutrients well.
  • Clay Soil: Holds nutrients but drains poorly.
  • Loamy Soil: A balanced mix of sand, silt, and clay, ideal for most plants.

For more detailed information, visit the NOAA Soil Types Guide.
