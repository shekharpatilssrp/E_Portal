Different Types of Soil

Sandy Soil

Sandy soil is gritty and light-colored, allowing water to drain easily. It's made of tiny pieces of rock and sand.

Loamy Soil

Loamy soil is a balanced mixture of sand, silt, and clay, making it ideal for most plants.

Clay Soil

Clay soil is dense and sticky when wet, retaining nutrients well but draining poorly.

Soil Care Tips

  • Watering: Ensure soil is moist but not waterlogged.
  • Soil Testing: Regularly test soil pH and nutrient levels.
  • Amendments: Add compost or organic matter to improve soil structure.
  • Crop Rotation: Rotate crops to prevent soil depletion.
