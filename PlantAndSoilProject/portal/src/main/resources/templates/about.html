About Us

Helping you understand your plants and soil better.

Who We Are

We are a passionate team of botanists, soil scientists, and developers committed to helping gardeners, farmers, and researchers better understand the interactions between soil and plants.

Our application allows users to test soil, track plant health, and get personalized suggestions based on real data.

Whether you're a backyard gardener or a professional grower, our platform makes soil science accessible and actionable.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower people with tools and knowledge to grow healthier plants through better soil understanding. We bridge the gap between environmental data and practical action for sustainable agriculture and gardening.
